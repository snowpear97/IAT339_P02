Welcome to LINGO!

Whether you are studying for a course, preparing for your next overseas trip, or wanting to become a polyglot, Lingo has got the language books just for you!

What is Lingo?

Lingo is an online bookstore that sells books for language learners specifically. There are many bookstores that sell various types of fiction and non fiction books, but our team wanted to make a specific business that sells books for people who are trying to learn a new language. Examples of products that could be sold are language dictionaries, textbooks for various different languages, and possibly media in other languages for consumption.

Our bookstore is also a source of information to potential customers, providing information for language learners, and in essence is a gateway to basic learning resources for language learners. While it may be smaller compared to big retailers like Amazon which sells almost everything, Lingo will have more specialized books on languages for language learners who want to go further, or linguists who want to study and dive deeper into a language.

Who is this Bookstore aimed for?

Lingo will provide a niche to language learners only. Since it is a hub where all types of books for language learning is available at, those who are dedicated to learning a language would possibly browse through the item listing selections available to see what books would fit their needs. Additionally, it is a shop recommended to professors or teachers who are teaching the language, as it provides a resource for students to gather all relevant material for study of another language.
