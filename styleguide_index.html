Branding

Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do?

The idea behind the company I want to create is a bookstore that sells books for language learners specifically. There are many bookstores that sell various types of fiction and non fiction books, but I want to make a specific business that sells books for people who are trying to learn a new language. Examples of products that could be sold are language dictionaries, and textbooks for various different langauges, and possibly media in other languages for consumption.

Who is the company to potential customers?

The company is a source of information to potential customers. The compancy provides informations for language learners, and in essence the company is a gateway to basic learning resources for language learners. While it may be smaller compared to big retailers like Amazon which sells everything, the company will have more specialized books on languages for language learners who want to go further, or linguistist who wants to study the language in detail.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

This company will provide a niche to language learners only. Since it is a hub where all types of books for language learning is avaliable at, those who are dedicated to learning a language would possibly browse through the selection avaliable to see what books would fit their needs. Possibly, it may be recommended by professors or teachers who are teaching the language, as it provides a resource for students to gather all relevant material for study of another language.
